3/ Get a private Gist URL

A gist is a sort of post-it note that you can share, share it with us!

You have to :

  1. create a private gist (no login required)
  2. Name it like this : webperfcontest2010-name
  3. Clic on "create private gist"
  4. Copy paste the generated url in the register form

During the contest, while modifying the sources, note in this Gist all the modifications you are doing.

This list of modifications will help us when judging :

  • to settle candidates with very close times / sizes ( < 30ms, < 5kb)
  • to detect potential cheaters

Gist is a free service of Github, gists are small pieces of text/code that you can share with people easily. You dont have to register to Github to use it, dont worry

You will receive an FTP account tonight or tomorrow. By then you can start your optimizations on your side (local machine, your own server ...). We will send you your FTP credentials and all the info you need to put your files online.
