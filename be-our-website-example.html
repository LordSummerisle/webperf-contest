Website owner, we need you!

We are a group of web performance professionnals organizing a web performance contest with the help of our sponsors YOTTAA & Alwaysdata.

A web performance contest ?

When the contest starts (25th October) we give participants a ZIP with a complete real world web page example in it.

They do their best to optimize it and send their modifications on our sponsor's servers (provided by Alwaysdata).

When the contest ends (30th November), we rank particpant's urls and decide of winners.

Why we need your website

For the contest to be really interesting, we are in the search of real-world websites examples to optimize.

Altough we already have some candidates, we are still searching for example websites that could fit : high trafic, lots of css and javascript, willing to be optimized!

Do you own such a website or do you work for ? Read on.

Benefits for you

  • Having the best advices from multiple professionnals about how to speed up your website.
  • Complete examples with comments & how it was done, not just a "boring" PDF analysis of the number of seconds it takes to load your site.
  • Do you know how such information costs today ? A lot ! Having it for free is a great opportunity.

Risks

None!

What! Everyone will be able to see my source code, I'm in DANGER!

No! Participants will only be given static assets, there's no need for you to give us your server's side source code.

Also if you do not know how to give us static version of your pages, no problem we can do it for you.
