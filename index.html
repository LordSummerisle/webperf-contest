Brief

We are a group of web performance junkies. We are planning a contest like the js1k competition but this time on the subject of WPO (Web Performance Optimization).

This is the first time a competition of this genre is organized (well, almost).

The contest will start the 18th October at 12:00 am GMT+1 and end the 30th November at 08:00pm GMT+1.

How to enter the competition ?

When the contest start, people will be able to download a zip from this website. The zip will contain a complete static webpage (.html) with all the required assets (js, css, backgrounds, ads) for the page to work.

Each participants will receive an FTP account to put their files. Of course you won't be able to see other participants urls before the end of the contest (also this removes ability to cheat with other people's optimized assets). The FTP accounts will be active for the time of the contest.

Participants will be able to access their files from 3 different domains, to maximize possibilities.

Each participant will provide their best magic skills to make the example webpage fast.

How to win ?

When the contest ends, each participant's url will be tested in multiple tools and ranked by theses categories :

  • Best YOTTAA score
  • Best sum of YSLOW, Pagespeed & Zoompf
  • Shortest loading time (start render + onload)
  • Best progressive rendering
  • Smallest website size, tiniest number of requests
  • Innovation award / best idea

What to win ?

Fame, self accomplishement & gifts if our sponsors are generous !

Rules

Do not cheat ! Making a complete JPG of the webpage is cheating (even if you strip exif data, yes!).
